Alpine Trail Ridge Inn Estes Park Lodging on a Budget

Jay and Fran Grooters welcome you to the Alpine Trail Ridge Inn. It is a seasonal roadside motor inn located on US Highway 36 West near the South entrance to Rocky Mountain National Park. The Inn is open May 1st through October 5th, but reservations can be made throughout the year for the current and next season. Alpine Trail Ridge Inn and the Sundeck Restaurant have been independently owned and operated for over 50 years.

We are ranked #2 of 30 hotels in Estes Park in the POPULARITY INDEX rating on TripAdvisor.com. See our nearly all positive reviews on TripAdvisor.com. We have made some changes and have more planned for next year based upon some of the input we have received from our reviews and from our other guests.

At Alpine Trail Ridge Inn, you'll find excellent Estes Park lodging value and personalized care -- all the elements for a memorable vacation. We specialize in customized hiking information.

All 48 rooms are non-smoking, and no pets. They are always exceptionally clean, comfortable, and a great value. We are an older property, recently remodeled and well-maintained, with almost all of the chain amenities except for a hot tub or continental breakfast. We do have a nice heated outdoor pool.

Our guests return year after year because they always find a welcoming staff, excellent food, and comfortable, immaculate Estes Park lodging. We believe some things should never change.

For computer users, we offer free wireless internet connection in every room. (You will need a laptop with built-in wireless or a PCI card with installed software. It requires wireless system 802.11B or 802.11G)

We also provide a Free Business Center in the lobby with two computers with high-speed internet access and two printers, which are available for our guests to use at no charge. Fax service is also available.

Our 18 rooms with the private balconies offer the most Spectacular mountain views . All rooms have private entrances, and there is parking by the room on all but 7 rooms. We have rooms with King, Queen, and Double beds, and there are four Two Bedroom Family Units.

In each room, you will find individual climate control and private bathrooms . For dining, the Sundeck Restaurant is on property.

To clarify a few of the published comments, we definitely are a roadside motel, right next to US 36. There is traffic noise during the day, but it is noticeable in the rooms only if you have the sliding glass door or the front windows open. The comments on TripAdvisor about the size of the rooms being small are from the 1/3 of our rooms which are our Western rooms in our Center Building. They are an older motif, with knotty pine wainscoting and smaller rooms. Our Standard and Balcony rooms are much larger and more modern feeling. Square footages are listed with the room type descriptions.

We strive to have no problems at all, but If there is any type of problem, we ask our guests to contact the Front Desk and we will do our best to solve the situation to everyone's satisfaction.

Our goal is to provide the best accommodations and service for our guests, and to help them have a positively memorable stay in Estes Park.
